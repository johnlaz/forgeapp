Brainstorm — Bosch Microwave Repair
The magnetron tests fine on continuity. What else could cause no heating?
Given your BOM shows the magnetron is ruled out, the next suspects are the high-voltage diode and capacitor. The diode commonly fails open or short — test with your multimeter on the diode setting. The capacitor holds lethal charge even unplugged; discharge it through a 10kΩ resistor before touching. Also check the thermal cutout fuse on the magnetron — these pop silently.
How do I safely discharge the cap?
Use a 10kΩ, 5W resistor bridged across both capacitor terminals with insulated probes. Hold for 10 seconds. The resistor limits inrush current so you don't get a violent arc. Verify with your meter at the <2V setting afterward. Adding this to your log as a safety step is a good idea.

Getting Started

Up and running in 60 seconds

01
Open the app

Launch LazForge in any modern browser. A sample project is pre-loaded on first visit so you can explore immediately.

02
Add your key

Paste your free Groq API key in Settings. That's the only credential needed — everything else is local.

03
Create a project

Pick a template, give it a name, and start logging. Add BOM items, link manuals to the vault, and track stages.

04
Install as PWA

Hit "Add to Home Screen" from your browser to install LazForge as a native app. Works offline, no app store needed.

🔒 Your data never leaves your device

LazForge stores everything in your browser's localStorage. No account, no server, no cloud sync. Your projects, logs, and parts lists belong to you — period. Export any time via print or AI Compile.
